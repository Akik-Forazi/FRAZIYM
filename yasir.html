FRAZIYM TECH & AI · 2026

Yasir Arafat is the Co-founder and Chief Creative Officer of FRAZIYM TECH & AI. His role is to make sure FRAZIYM doesn't just build extraordinary technology — but communicates it in a way that is felt as well as understood.

As CCO, Yasir owns the full creative output of the company: visual identity, brand voice, product storytelling, design systems, and the way FRAZIYM presents itself to the world. He works alongside Akik and Sakibul to ensure that the technical depth of XORZENX and AXONIX-ZERO is never buried under jargon — and never oversimplified into empty promises either.

Good creative direction in an AI company isn't decoration — it's trust. Every design decision, every word choice, every interface moment is an opportunity to demonstrate that FRAZIYM takes its mission seriously. Yasir makes sure that opportunity is never wasted.

"The best technology in the world fails if nobody understands why it matters. That's what we're here to fix."

— Yasir Arafat

What the CCO shapes at FRAZIYM

Brand Identity

The FRAZIYM visual system, logo mark, color language, and typography are all intentional. They signal precision, depth, and a local-first philosophy without needing to say any of it explicitly.

Product Storytelling
