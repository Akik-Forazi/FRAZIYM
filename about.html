About · 2026

Built on conviction

FRAZIYM TECH & AI is a pre-seed AI lab founded by Akik Faraji, Sakibul Hassan, and Yasir Arafat — built out of Dhaka, Bangladesh. We build autonomous AI systems that run entirely on your hardware — no subscriptions, no data leaks, no black boxes.

Mission

Build the most trusted local AI infrastructure — delivering big-tech performance without surrendering privacy, transparency, or control.

The AI industry is moving fast, but much of it trades user control for convenience. We believe enterprises deserve autonomous systems that run inside their security perimeter, explain every decision they make, and can be audited, modified, or shut down at any moment. That is what FRAZIYM is building.

Principles

How we build

🔒

Local-first, always

Every system is designed to run inside your perimeter. Cloud backends are an option, not a requirement. Your data is yours.

🔍

Radical transparency

We ship with diagnostics on by default. Session history, token streaming, and debug logging are built in. If it's in beta, we say so.

⚡

Speed without shortcuts

We move fast but never cut corners on security, reliability, or user control. Loop detection, atomic writes, workspace sandboxing — production details matter from day one.

🧱

Systems thinking

Every component has a clear interface. Backends are interchangeable. Tools are composable. The loop engine is independent. Nothing is monolithic.

🎯

Uncompromising quality

Typed errors, structured logging, schema validation, atomic config writes, sandboxed workspace access. Production practices are non-negotiable even in beta.

🌍

Public accountability

We publish our stage, our roadmap, and our known limitations. We earn trust through demonstrated reliability, not marketing claims.

Portfolio

What we are building

A portfolio of systems that blend private compute, multi-model orchestration, and premium developer experience. Every product is designed to ship into real enterprise workflows.
