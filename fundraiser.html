About XORZEN

What makes this different

⚡

Sparse Activation

90% fewer parameters active per token. Dense models fire everything for every token — XORZEN uses targeted, adaptive routing that produces the same output at a fraction of the cost.

💡

HASS Architecture

Hierarchical Adaptive Sparse Stacks with Mixture-of-Experts routing. Latent Chain-of-Thought reasoning built-in. 5× RAM reduction, 20× CPU speedup on matched benchmarks.

🌍

Accessible AI

ChatGPT costs $20/month — that's luxury pricing for 500M+ people worldwide. If XORZEN scales as the math suggests: $2/month with 70% margins. Better economics means accessible to everyone.

The Founder's Reality

I failed my HSC (high school) exams in 2025 because I spent those 8 months building XORZEN instead of studying. My family wants me to retake them. The education system here doesn't understand why someone would choose architecture over credentials.

The prototypes work. The math checks out. Small models from 1M–10M parameters show the sparse activation architecture delivers real compute savings. The routing works. The MoE structure works. I've written 50,000+ lines of code.

I'm training on a laptop CPU. The full 277M parameter model would take months on this hardware. GPUs would cut that to days. This is the only thing standing between the prototype and proof-of-scale.
