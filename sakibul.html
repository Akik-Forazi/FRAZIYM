Profile

The CEO behind FRAZIYM

Sakibul Hassan

Director & CEO

FRAZIYM TECH & AI · 2026

Sakibul Hassan is the CEO of FRAZIYM TECH & AI. He joined the company at its founding and is growing into his leadership role as the company matures. The technical foundation is being built by Akik Faraji — Sakibul's domain is everything that turns that technology into a company: strategy, partnerships, operations, and eventually investor relations and go-to-market.

Like everyone at FRAZIYM right now, Sakibul is not yet operating full time on the company. He is learning the responsibilities of the CEO role in parallel with the company's own growth — an honest position for an early-stage pre-seed company where the foundation is still being laid.

His focus as the company advances is on building the business infrastructure: legal registration, commercial strategy, early enterprise relationships, and ensuring that when FRAZIYM is ready to grow, the operational foundation is in place to support it.

"The technology Akik has built is extraordinary. My job is to make sure the world gets to use it."

— Sakibul Hassan

The Mission

What FRAZIYM is building toward

Sakibul's strategic lens is focused on one goal: making sure FRAZIYM's AI technology reaches the businesses and individuals who need it — without compromising on local-first principles, privacy, or technical integrity.

🎯

Local-First by Principle

FRAZIYM builds AI that runs on your hardware, not ours. Sakibul ensures that business model and go-to-market strategy always preserves this — no compromise for growth metrics.

🤝

Enterprise Without Selling Out

The path to enterprise adoption is through trust, not lock-in. Sakibul is building a commercial strategy where customers win by owning their own AI stack.

🌍

Built from Bangladesh

FRAZIYM proves that world-class AI doesn't require Silicon Valley. Sakibul's leadership helps tell that story and open doors that geography might otherwise close.
